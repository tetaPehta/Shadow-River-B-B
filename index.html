THE FARM

~Traditional Farm-stay~

We hold 100+ sheep on our farm. In the summer they graze open hills and are free to roam. When visiting the farm You will meet ducks, hens, pet boar: George, companion horse; Bobby, jack the donkey and many more!

Farm is open for visitors from St. Patrick's Day onwards.
